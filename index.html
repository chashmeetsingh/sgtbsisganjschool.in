About the school

The School is situated in the heart of Chandni Chowk near Fountain. It is situated in a pucca building containing sufficient space for the Class Rooms, Labs, Office, Staff Room, Medical Room, Library, Band Room, Domestic Science Room and Babajiâ€™s Room.
The School was first established in 1945 as a Primary School. It was upgraded as a Senior Secondary School in 1977.


STREAMS OFFERED

Humanities and Commerce streams are being offered to students at +2 levels in the school.

SCHOOL BAND

Bag Pipe band of about 70 students, no doubt unique in Delhi, gave a beautiful performance. The band has been participating in Republic Day and bagging award for last 35 years.

It has been winning 1st prize in Delhi State Band Competition and receiving Cash/ Trophies at various levels.

HOUSES

The whole school is divided into four houses namely Ajit Singh House, Jujhar Singh House, Jorawar Singh House and Fateh Singh House.
Inter house competitions in debate, poetry, recitation, extempore speech are held from time to time to promote students for participation in Zonal as well as State Level Competitions.
Prizes are awarded to winning house and the individual students.

GAMES AND SPORTS

Games and sports play an important role in the growth of a child. In spite of lack of proper play ground, every year the school takes part in the Zonal as well as inter Zonal competitions.

The school participates in the following games and sports:

  • Kho-Kho
  • Athletics
  • Skipping
  • Badminton
  • Carrom Board
  • Ludo
